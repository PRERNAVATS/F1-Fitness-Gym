The F1 Fitness Engine

F1 Fitness was founded with a singular vision: to bring a world-class, high-octane fitness training facility to the heart of Crossings Republik, Ghaziabad. We designed a space where passion meets precision, combining advanced biomechanically engineered machinery with elite coaching.

Whether you are a beginner stepping onto a gym floor for the first time, or an advanced athlete aiming to shatter plateaus, our gym is built to serve your journey. We offer an inviting, motivating environment equipped with dumbbells up to 60kg, a dedicated turf track, and multi-functional cardio machines.

  • Spacious 2nd-floor layout in Orbit Plaza
  • Commercial-grade strength & plate-loaded equipment
  • Flexible plans with zero hidden charges
  • open till 11:00 PM to fit busy schedules
